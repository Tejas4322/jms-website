About JMS Industries

Engineering Excellence Since 2009

Our Story

JMS Industries is a trusted leader in the Indian elevator industry, with over 15 years of engineering excellence. We deliver premium end-to-end solutions, from precision manufacturing and seamless assembly to dedicated after-sales service. Every elevator is thoughtfully customized to enhance architectural vision while ensuring safety, reliability, and refined craftsmanship.

Our commitment to quality and innovation drives us to create mobility experiences that blend performance with design. Built on trust and exceptional service, JMS Industries continues to redefine vertical transportation, offering elevators that elevate lifestyles and set new benchmarks in reliability, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Commitment

JMS Industries is committed to being a reliable manufacturing partner to the elevator industry. We measure success not just by output, but by how well our components perform within our customers' systems, over time, across projects, and through repeated production cycles.

By combining experience, precision manufacturing, and a quality-first mindset, we continue to support the elevator industry with components engineered for long-lasting performance and trust.
