Elevator Car Frame

Structural Stability and Load Support for Elevator Systems

JMS Industries manufactures robust elevator car frames designed to provide structural stability, load support, and safe integration within elevator systems. Our car frames are fabricated using controlled processes to ensure dimensional accuracy and long-term reliability.

Subcategories

Car Frame - Geared

Designed for geared elevator systems, these frames offer dependable load-bearing performance and structural rigidity.

Car Frame - Gearless

Optimized for modern gearless elevator configurations, ensuring strength with precise alignment.

Dumbwaiter Car Frame

Compact, lightweight frames engineered for small service lifts and material handling systems.

Car Lift Car Frame

Heavy-duty frames designed to support vehicle loads with enhanced structural reinforcement.

Goods Lift Car Frame

Built for industrial and commercial goods elevators, focusing on strength, durability, and stability.

Applications

Elevator car frames manufactured by JMS Industries are used in:

  • Passenger elevators
  • Commercial, residential, and high-rise buildings
  • Goods and freight elevators
  • Car lift systems
  • Service and Dumbwaiters elevators
  • Hospital Lifts
