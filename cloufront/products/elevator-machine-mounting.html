Elevator Machine Mounting

Structural Stability and Vibration Control

At JMS Industries, we manufacture machine mounting components designed to support and securely position elevator machinery within elevator systems. These components play a critical role in maintaining structural stability, accurate alignment, and vibration control, contributing to the reliable operation of the elevator equipment over its service life.

All machine mounting components are manufactured to customer drawings and technical specifications, ensuring compatibility with varied elevator configurations and load requirements.

Technical Specifications

Parameter Specification
Material Mild Steel (MS)
Component Configuration Machine Mounting Frame / Base Plate / Bracket
Alignment Requirement High-precision mounting
Vibration Consideration Structural stability-focused
Dimensions As per the customer design
Manufacturing Process Fabrication, Welding, Finishing

Load Handling As per the customer's requirement

Note: Specifications may vary based on machine type, load conditions, and elevator configuration.

Applications

Machine mounting components manufactured by JMS Industries are used in:

  • Passenger elevator systems
  • Commercial and high-rise elevators
  • Heavy-duty and special-purpose elevators
